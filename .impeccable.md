## Design Context

### Users
London cycle-hire commuters checking dock availability and journey status while travelling. The interface must communicate current state quickly, often one-handed and at a glance.

### Brand Personality
Native, modern, and dependable. The experience should feel calm and polished while retaining enough visual energy to make live information immediately recognizable.

### Aesthetic Direction
Follow current Apple platform conventions and Human Interface Guidelines, with restrained Liquid Glass treatments for important live surfaces. Support light and dark appearances, Dynamic Type, accessibility, and reduced motion. Preserve the established red, blue, and gray availability colors.

### Design Principles
- Make live status and the next useful action understandable at a glance.
- Prefer native controls, materials, typography, navigation, and interaction behavior.
- Reserve emphasis, glass, glow, and motion for genuinely active or time-sensitive information.
- Keep motion subtle, performant, and nonessential; disable repeating motion when Reduce Motion is enabled.
- Maintain strong contrast and touch targets for quick one-handed use while travelling.
